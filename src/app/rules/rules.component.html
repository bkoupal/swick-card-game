♠♥ The Rules of SWICK ♣♦

A Classic Card Game

style The Deck

Standard deck without 2's through 6's. No Jokers. Total cards: 32

Ace is highest card, 7 is lowest card except when played per card values below.

7 8 9 10 J Q K A

account_balance_wallet The Pot

  • Minimum 12 cents - Starting pot value with minimum 3 people playing
  • Minimum ante is 3 cents per person including dealer
  • Dealer always antes 3 cents more than ante value
  • Pot must always be divisible by 3
Example: Player 1 = 3¢, Player 2 = 3¢, Player 3 (Dealer) = 6¢
Total pot = 12¢ (Three 4¢ Tricks)

emoji_events Objective of Game

Win tricks by playing cards that beat other hands. Each trick is worth 1/3 of the pot.

Winning a Trick: A Trick is 1/3 value of the pot. No limit on size of pot.

Going Set: Players go Set if they do not win at least one trick. They must match the pot for the next hand.

stars Hand Values - Highest to Lowest

1st 3 Aces - Any suit
2nd 3 Sevens - Any suit
3rd Ace, King, Queen of Trump

play_circle Beginning the Game

  1. 3-6 players sit around table
  2. Shuffle deck 3 times
  3. Deal one card at a time face up to each player
  4. First person to get an Ace becomes the dealer
  5. Dealer announces the ante amount (must make pot divisible by 3)
  6. Players ante in to join the hand

style The Deal

  1. Dealer shuffles deck minimum 3 times
  2. Person to dealer's right cuts the deck
  3. Dealer deals 3 cards face down to each player (left to right)
  4. After all players have 3 cards, dealer turns over the next card - this is the Trump card
  5. Dealer decides to either:
    • Place Trump card face up on table (everyone sees it, dealer doesn't keep it)
    • Keep Trump card as part of their hand (must use it, cannot discard it)

casino Play Begins

  1. All players except dealer pick up their 3 cards
  2. Dealer asks each player (starting left): "Are you in?"
  3. Players respond by:
    • Knocking on table = I'm in
    • "I Pass" = I'm out (lose ante)
  4. Dealer asks players who are in: "How many cards would you like?"
  5. Players may discard up to 3 cards and draw replacements
  6. Dealer then looks at their cards and may also discard/draw
  7. If dealer kept Trump card and draws 3 new cards, they must discard 1 to end with 3 cards

trending_up Playing Tricks

First Card Played:

  • First player (left of dealer) plays a card face up
  • Special Rule: If you have Ace of Trump, you MUST lead with it on the first play
  • If no Ace of Trump, play any card

Following Players Must:

  1. Follow suit if possible (play same suit)
  2. Beat the card if you can (play higher card of same suit)
  3. If you can't follow suit, play a Trump card if you have one
  4. If you can't follow suit or Trump, play any card

Winning the Trick:

Highest card of the suit led wins, UNLESS a Trump card is played - then highest Trump wins.

Winner of the trick leads the next round.

Play continues until all 3 cards are played (3 tricks total).

warning Special Rules for Dealer Keeping Trump Card

If Dealer Keeps Ace, King, Queen, or Jack Trump:

Must win 2 Tricks or go Set Double (match pot twice)

If dealer wins only 1 Trick, they keep that amount but must pay double the pot for next hand.

If Dealer Keeps 10, 9, 8, or 7 Trump:

Must win 1 Trick or go Set Single (match pot once)

Set Single Option:

Dealer may choose to go "Set Single" immediately after drawing - don't play the hand, just match the pot.

money_off Going Set

A player goes Set when they don't win at least one Trick.

  • Player must match the pot value for the next hand
  • Players who passed before playing do NOT pay for next hand (free ride)
  • Dealer always antes 3 cents for next hand
  • If dealer kept a face Trump card (A, K, Q, J) and won only 1 Trick, they go Set Double (pay twice the pot)

flag Ending a Game

Players agree to play to a specific time or person.

Important: No game shall end when there is a Set still in place. The Set must be broken (played out where pot is divided).

list Quick Reference

Players: 3-6
Cards per Player: 3
Minimum Pot: 12¢
Minimum Ante: 3¢
Dealer Extra: +3¢
Tricks per Hand: 3

Have Fun Playing SWICK!
